Frequently Asked Questions

What is the referral code?
Your referral code is provided by your onboarding agent after you've been pre-qualified. It links your application to your agent and unlocks the next step of onboarding.
What kind of data does Nielsen Pulse collect?
The Nielsen Pulse app passively measures which apps and sites you use. It does not capture passwords, messages, or personal content. Full disclosure is shown during setup.
How much can I earn?
Rewards vary based on participation, device, and the platform's terms. Your dashboard shows current earnings any time.
Who qualifies?
US residents 18+ with a supported iPhone, iPad, or Android device. Your onboarding agent confirms eligibility before issuing a referral code.
Can I leave the panel anytime?
Yes. Remove the app from your phone at any time. No cancellation fees, no commitments.
